ABOUT US

Janak Education Materials Center Ltd. is one of the wings of Government of Nepal. Teachers were trained on a massive scale and this centre came into its name and since then it has been known as Janak Education Materials Centre. It was established 29 years ago, following the dawn of democracy in Nepal in 1950 A.D., a well-conceived educational programme was launched. Finally in the year 1966, the name of the Epic king "Janak" was prefixed before its name.
